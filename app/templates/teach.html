The Teaching Zone

We're so glad that you want to tutor students. It is incredibly rewarding, and can even be fun! Keep in mind that these students are relying on you for help - as such, it is not a light responsibility. It is your job to show up to every session prepared and ready to engage your student to your utmost.

When you fill out and submit this form, you'll see a page showing requests from students for only the classes that you can help with. More precisely, if their request contains any classes that you've said you can help with, you'll see it. You'll also see a sample email - we encourage you to reach out to the student first, and from there build a relationship.
